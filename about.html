Sottile's Pizza Werribee

Sottile's Pizza & Takeaway in Werribee was founded in 1988. With many years of experience in the industry they have perfected the art of delivering hearty, delicious pizzas. Using only fresh ingredients of the highest quality they not only produce a wide range of mouth-watering pizzas but they also serve freshly made Pasta, Steak, Seafood & Chicken.

Drop into our store & our friendly staff will do the rest…
